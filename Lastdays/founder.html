District Elder Arthur R. Johnson

Our Founder

Sunrise: July 17, 1950 ~ Sunset: May 29, 2010

Beloved Man of God, husband, father, son, brother, uncle, nephew, writer, musician, humanitarian and friend.

Although he is now gone on, awaiting the dead in Christ to rise, we will always love, adore him, and cherish his memory!

To some he was Pastor. To some he was Elder. To some he was Big Daddy. But we all will remember him as a TRUE MAN OF GOD!!!!!!
